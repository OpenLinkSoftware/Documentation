==<nop>RuleBook Checker==

The <nop>OpenLink Rules Book Checker ships with <nop>OpenLink's <nop>WinNT server components. This utility reads the information contained in your Rules Book and displays portions of the Rules Book which are not syntactically correct. (Only logic is examined. Actual values are not.) Finally, your Rules Book Checker should appear in your <nop>OpenLink folder. The <nop>OpenLink folder is contained in the Programs folder attached to your Startup Menu.

 [[OpenLink]]  Virtuoso is a commercial quality SQL/XML server foundation that connects databases, presents a unified data model, and creates composite applications through Web Services tools. Virtuoso Universal Server gathers, integrates, and creates Web Based services in cooperation with existing enterprise IT system architectures, preserving stability of existing systems and clearing a path to the future of Service Oriented Architecture.

==<nop>KeepAliveTimeout==

The <nop>KeepAliveTimeout parameter appears in the [<nop>HTTPServer] section of our virtuoso.ini file. <nop>KeepAliveTimeout passes the number of seconds, which Virtuoso will wait before it closes an idle HTTP 1.1 connection.

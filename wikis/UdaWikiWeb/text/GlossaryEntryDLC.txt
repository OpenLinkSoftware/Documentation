==Glossary of Technical Terms==

==DLC==

The DLC environment variable appears in the [Environment PROGRESSXX] section of the <nop>OpenLink Session Rules Book. This variable must be set to the root directory of your Progress installation. 

Note: This variable may be set and exported from your Administrative Assistant's Database Agent Settings Form or Wizard. 

Example: {{{DLC=/dbs/progress}}}

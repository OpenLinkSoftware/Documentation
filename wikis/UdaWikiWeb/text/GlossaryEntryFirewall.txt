==Firewall==

Firewalls are comprised of hardware and/or software components. These components protect private networks from unsolicited, public network traffic. <nop>OpenLink's Multi-Tier drivers can operate through a firewall. However, TCP Ports High through Low. You may find the values--which are passed by <nop>PortHigh and <nop>PortLow--in the [Protocol TCP] section of the OpenLink Session Rules Book. 

Note: <nop>OpenLink's 1.x and 3.x users must also open UDP port #60001 on their firewalls. Use of UDP port #60001 has been deprecated in more recent builds.

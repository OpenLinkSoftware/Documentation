===ODBC Loaded and ODBC Unloaded===

Do you encounter "ODBC Loaded" and "ODBC Unloaded" dialog boxes each time an ODBC driver is loaded or Unloaded? If so, take the following action:

1. Expand your Windows Start Menu.
2. Expand your Programs menu.
3. Expand your <nop>OpenLink folder.
4. Click the <nop>OpenLink Client Configuration Utility.Go
5. Click the utility's ODBC tab.
6. Uncheck the "Show load and unload" box.
7. Click OK.
8. Exit the utility.
9. Test.

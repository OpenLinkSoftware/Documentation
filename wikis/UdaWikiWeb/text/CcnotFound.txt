===cc: not found===

If you want to relink <nop>OpenLink's Database Agents, you must insure that a C compiler is installed on your machine. If a C compiler is not compiled on your machine, you will receive "cc: not found" errors. If you receive these errors, obtain the appropriate compiler before proceeding.

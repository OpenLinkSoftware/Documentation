==<nop>ServerPort==

[1] The <nop>ServerPort parameter appears in the [Parameters] section of your virtuoso.ini file. <nop>ServerPort passes the IP port number on which your Virtuoso server starts listening. 

[2] The <nop>ServerPort parameter appears in the [HTTPServer] section of your virtuoso.ini file. Here, <nop>ServerPort passes the IP port number on which your VSP server starts listening.

==Glossary of Technical Terms==

==demo-stop.sh==

demo-stop.sh appears in the /bin directory of Unix-based Virtuoso installations. You may use this script to stop the Virtuoso Demo server.   

Example: 
  
{{{
./demo-stop.sh   
}}}

See Also: demo-start.sh

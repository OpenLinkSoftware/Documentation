===The open database table is full, increase the -h parameter. (1415)===

 "The open database table is full" error message is Progress specific. It occurs when Progress cannot open additional database tables. To proceed, you must close connections to one or more database tables. Alternatively, use the {{{-h}}} session parameter to increase your available Progress resources. Your Progress documentation will provide you will a full account of the {{{-h}}} client option.

==<nop>ErrorLogFile==

The <nop>ErrorLogFile parameter appears in the [Database] section of your virtuoso.ini file. This parameter passes the name of the file, which will capture your database error messages. Product Support or your DBA may examine these files for the nature and cause of error conditions.

==Glossary of Technical Terms==

==demo-start.sh==

demo-start.sh appears in the /bin directory of Unix-based Virtuoso installations. You may use this script to start the Virtuoso Demo server. Be advised. You may be prompted to pass the demo.ini file at startup.   

Example: 
  
{{{
./demo-start.sh -c /usr/virtuoso/demo/demo.ini 
}}}

See Also: demo-stop.sh

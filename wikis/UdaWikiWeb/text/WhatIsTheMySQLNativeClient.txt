==Understanding the <nop>MySQL native client ==

<nop>OpenLink's products do not rely on a <nop>MySQL client layer to establish connections to <nop>MySQL. <nop>OpenLink's <nop>MySQL drivers are wire protocol drivers. Hence, <nop>OpenLink Support Staff do not track the existence of a <nop>MySQL native client.

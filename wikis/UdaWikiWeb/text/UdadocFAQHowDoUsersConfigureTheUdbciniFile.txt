*Open the client-side Administrative Assistant in a Web browser.  
*Expand the *Client Components Administration* menu.  
*Expand the *Data Source Name Configuration* menu.  
*Expand the **Edit Data Sources by Wizard ** menu.  
*Click **Edit UDBC Data Sources ** .  
*Click **Add ** .  
*Answer the questions posed by the Wizard.  
*Save the DSN and test.

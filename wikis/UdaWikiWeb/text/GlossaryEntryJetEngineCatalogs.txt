==Jet Engine Catalogs==

The Jet Engine Catalogs checkbox appears in the Administrative Assistant's Database Agent Settings Form and Wizard. This option facilitates translation of certain datatypes by the Microsoft Jet Engine. If you utilize MS applications, and you notice that money and other datatypes are mishandled, check the Jet Engine Catalogs box. 

Be advised. Jet Engine Catalogs is not supported by all database agents. Contact <nop>OpenLink Product Support for a list of agents which employ this option.

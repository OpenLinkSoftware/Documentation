===A license is required for this agent===


<nop>OpenLink's Database Agents require licenses. If you experience "A license is required" errors, contact your <nop>OpenLink Account Manager. Your Account Manager will provide you with the appropriate license file.

Be advised - If you have a current license file, be sure to place that license file in the {{{/bin/}}} sub-directory of your <nop>OpenLink server components installation. Otherwise, your license may not be detected. Likewise ensure that the <nop>OpenLink {{{/bin/}}} sub-directory appears in your {{{PATH}}}.

* Database Agent Version String 
* License File

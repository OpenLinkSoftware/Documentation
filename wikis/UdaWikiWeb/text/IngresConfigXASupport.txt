==Configuring Ingres for XA support ==

XA is supported with Ingres II version 2.0 and higher and Ingres II DBMS does not require any additional configuration for XA support to be enabled.

The Ingres II XA support was tested against the following versions:

* Ingres II v2.0

* Ingres II v2.6

* Ingres II v3.0 (Open release)

==Protocols==

The Protocols parameter appears in the [Request Broker] section of the <nop>OpenLink Session Rules Book. You may use Protocols to specify the network protocols which you will be using to establish your connections. 

Example: 

{{{
Protocols = tcp 
}}}

See Also: SPX, TCP

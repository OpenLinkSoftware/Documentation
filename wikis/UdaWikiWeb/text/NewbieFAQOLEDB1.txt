==Beginner UDA Questions==

===What do your OLE DB Providers do?=== 

<nop>OpenLink's OLE DB providers provide OLE DB data access to ODBC data sources via a bridge to an <nop>OpenLink or non-<nop>OpenLink ODBC driver. <nop>OpenLink provides 32bit and 64bit providers for Windows x86, Opteron, and Itanium systems.

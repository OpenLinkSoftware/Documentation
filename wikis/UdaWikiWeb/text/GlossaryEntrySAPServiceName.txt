==SAP Service Name==

The SAP Service Name is the name which is encoded in SAP registration packets which are forwarded by a SPX-enabled Broker. The default value is OPENLINK. However, you may change this value, if it conflicts with another service name.   

You will find a SAP Service Name field on the Edit Request Broker's Parameters Form which appears in the Administrative Assistant. You will also find a <nop>SAPServiceName parameter in the [Protocol SPX]section of the <nop>OpenLink Session Rules Book.

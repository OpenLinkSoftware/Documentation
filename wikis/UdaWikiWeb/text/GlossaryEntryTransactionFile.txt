==<nop>TransactionFile==

The <nop>TransactionFile parameter occurs in the [Database] section of your virtuoso.ini file. This parameter passes the name of your Virtuoso transaction (.trx) file. Be advised. If this parameter is omitted, your database will cease to log its transactions. Moreover, if your database terminates in an abnormal manner, you will not be able to recover data which was committed after your last checkpoint.

==<nop>ErrorLogLevel==

The <nop>ErrorLogLevel parameter appears in the [Database] section of your virtuoso.ini file. This parameter controls the quality and content of the messages logged during Virtuoso usage. To insure that you are capturing the maximum amount of debug data, set <nop>ErrorLogLevel to 7. 

See Also: <nop>ErrorLogFile

Tableview .dat and .pf Files

The tableview or "setup.p" process creates Progress .dat and a .pf files. Often, these files are named tableview.dat and tableview.pf. Moreover, the files usually appear in the Lite32 (Single-Tier) or /bin sub-directory (Multi-Tier) of the OpenLink installation.

If you cannot identify your tableview files, use find (Unix) or the Windows Explorer to search for matching .dat and .pf files. Once you find these files, forward copies to OpenLink Product Support.

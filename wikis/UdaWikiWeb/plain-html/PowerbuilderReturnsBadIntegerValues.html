Application Specific Problems

Bad Integer Values

If your PowerBuilder applications return bad integer values, take the following action:

1. Click on the PowerBuilder "DB Profiles" icon.
2. Highlight the DSN which you use to establish your connection.
3. Click on "Edit."
4. Click on the "Transaction" tab.
5. Locate "Cursors."
6. Select "Cursor Library" from the Cursor Library drop down.
7. Hit "Apply."
8. Test.

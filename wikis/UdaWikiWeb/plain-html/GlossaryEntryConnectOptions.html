Connect Options

The Connect Options (ConnectOptions) parameter appears in the [generic] section in your OpenLink Session Rules Book, the Administrative Assistant, the odbc.ini file, and the udbc.ini file. You may pass any valid, database specific connection parameters in your Connect Options field. For instance, you may pass Progress sockets options. Alternatively, you may pass -S server_name to designate a particular SQLServer service.

Be advised. If you hardcode Connect Options into your Session Rules Book, the Request Broker will ignore options passed by your DSN's.

Example:ConnectOptions = -S pro8 -N tcp -H openlink

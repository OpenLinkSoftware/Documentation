Generic Quick Start Installation and Configuration

Installation

1. Download the driver installer.

2. Retrieve your OpenLink license file from email or your OpenLink ODS page.

3. Place the license file on your client machine.

4. Run the installer.

5. Accept the defaults.

6. Exit the installer.


Configuration

1. Open your ODBC Data Sources Administrator.

2. Click the System DSN tab.

3. Click Add.

4. Select the OpenLink driver.

5. Click Finish.

6. Fill in the relevant values.

7. Test your DSN.

8. Click Finish to save your DSN.

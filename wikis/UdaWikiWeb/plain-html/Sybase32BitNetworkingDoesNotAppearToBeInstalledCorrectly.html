Sybase 32Bit Networking Does Not Appear to be Installed Correctly

The "Sybase 32Bit Networking" error occurs when OpenLink's deprecated ct-lib and db-lib format Single-Tier drivers can not find or use Sybase Open Client software. You can not continue with these drivers, until the Sybase Open Client is installed on your PC client. You have three options:

1. Upgrade to OpenLink's latest wire protocol Single-Tier drivers that do not require use of the Sybase client.

2. Obtain the Sybase client to continue work with the deprecated drivers.

2. If you cannot use or obtain Sybase client software and require use of the deprecated drivers, you may use OpenLink's Multi-Tier software. In this instance, Multi-Tier software must be installed on both the client and Sybase database server.

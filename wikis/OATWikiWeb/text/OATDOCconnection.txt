---++ Objects

---+++ OAT.Connection(type, optionsObject)

Creates a Connection. <tt>type</tt> is one of the <tt>TYPE_constants</tt> (see below). Available options depend on connection <tt>type</tt>:

   * <tt> <b>TYPE_XMLA</b> </tt>
   * <tt>*endpoint*</tt> - ToBeDone
   * <tt>*dsn*</tt> - ToBeDone
   * <tt>*user*</tt> - ToBeDone
   * <tt>*password*</tt> - ToBeDone
   * <tt><b>TYPE_REST</b></tt> 
   * <tt>*url*</tt> - ToBeDone
   * <tt><b>TYPE_WSDL</b></tt> 
   * <tt>*url*</tt> - ToBeDone

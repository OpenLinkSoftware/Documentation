---+ OpenLink AJAX Toolkit (OAT)
    
---++ Documentation
Check our
[[http://wiki.usnet.private/dataspace/dav/wiki/OATWikiWeb/OATDOCIndex][OAT Documentation]]
    
---++ Download

You can download an OS-, Web-server- and database- independent installation archive from the SourceForge
[[http://sourceforge.net/project/platformdownload.php?group_id=168143][project site]].

You can also download the Virtuoso-optimized oat_dav.vad package from
[[http://download.openlinksw.com/packages/5.0/virtuoso/][our official download site]].

---++Live Demo

We have a
[[http://oat.openlinksw.com/][live demonstration]]
  of the various widgets and controls OAT provides, including the XMLA data-access connectivity. You can also check our
[[http://demo.openlinksw.com/oatdemo][demo location]].

For maximum effect when viewing this Toolkit demo, please set your desktop resolution to at least 1024x768 pixels.

Troubleshoot Facet Browsing Overflows

This is likely a browser-specific rendering issue. If you experience facet browsing overflows, provide the following information to OpenLink Product Support:

1. State whether or not the problem occurs with a publicly accessible service such as http://lod.openlinksw.com or an in-house service.
2. Provide recreation details when the service is publicly accessible.
3. Provide the browser version.
4. State on what operating system the problem is encountered.

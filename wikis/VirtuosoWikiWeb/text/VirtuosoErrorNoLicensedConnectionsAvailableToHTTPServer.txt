=Virtuoso Error Message: {{{There are no licensed connections available to the HTTP Server}}}=

This error message generally indicates <a href="http://docs.openlinksw.com/virtuoso/webserver.html#vspconf">a misconfiguration in <code>virtuoso.ini</code></a>, or the lack of a license file.

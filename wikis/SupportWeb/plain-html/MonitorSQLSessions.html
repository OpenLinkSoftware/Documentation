How do I monitor open database sessions associated with MS SQLServer ODBC connections?

Use the following steps to monitor open database sessions associated with MS SQLServer ODBC connections:

  1. Launch your SQLServer Enterprise Manager.
  2. Expand the Microsoft SQLServers menu tree.
  3. Expand the SQLServer Group menu tree.
  4. Expand the menu tree associated with you SQLServer instance.
  5. Expand the Management folder.
  6. Click Current Activity to see open database connections.

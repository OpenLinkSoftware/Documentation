---++  How do I monitor open database sessions associated with MS SQLServer ODBC connections?  %BR%

Use the following steps to monitor open database sessions associated with MS SQLServer ODBC connections: %BR%
%BR%
   1 Launch your SQLServer Enterprise Manager.
   1 Expand the Microsoft SQLServers menu tree.
   1 Expand the SQLServer Group menu tree.
   1 Expand the menu tree associated with you SQLServer instance.
   1 Expand the Management folder.
   1 Click Current Activity to see open database connections.

---++Apply 4.x-5.x Single-Tier License Files on Mac OS X

   1 Open your *Finder*.
   1 Click the *Applications* icon.
   1 Expand the *Utilities* menu tree.
   1 Double Click the *Terminal* icon.
   1 cd into the /Library/Application Support/openlink/bin directory.
   1 Retrieve your new <nop>OpenLink license file from an email or [[ODSBriefcaseLicenseStorage][ODS briefcase]].
   1 Transfer your license into /Library/Application Support/openlink/bin.

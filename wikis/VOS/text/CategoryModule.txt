This is a categody of all modules of all products developed in OpenLink. Click Ref-by link at the bottom of the page to find overview pages of particular modules. Every module may have its own category, the page of module usually contains a search result by this category.

To add a starting page of a module called SampleSubsystem of a product called SampleTool into category of all modules, place the following text at the end of the page:

<verbatim>
---+++ All topics of CategorySampleSubsystem

%SEARCH{"CategorySampleSubsystem" web="Main" scope="text" order="modified" nosearch="on" nototal="on"}%

CategorySampleSubsystem CategorySampleTool CategoryModule
</verbatim>

(of course, replace all occurences of SampleSubsystem and SampleTool with the WikiWord names of your actual module and product)

Link existing ODS user with Facebook account using the Profile Setup Feature

  1. Make sure the steps from the ODS Facebook Integration Administrators Guide are performed.
  2. Go to http://cname:port/ods .



  3. Click "Sign In"



  4. Enter your ODS user credential and click "Login":



  5. Go to Profile Edit ->Security->Facebook



  6. Click the "f connect" button
  7. In the open new window enter your Facebook account credentials.



  8. Click "Login"



  9. Click "Update"
  10. As result your ODS user will be connected to your Facebook user as shown:

Export ODS Addressbook Contacts

ODS Addressbook offers to be exported the contacts by content type:

  • vCard
  • FOAF
  • CSV

Here is sample scenario:

  1. Go to http://cname:port/ods
  2. Log in as user.
  3. Go to Addressbook
  4. Click the "Export" button from the Addressbook Main horizontal navigation.
  5. Choose a Content type and enter Filename
  6. Click the "Export" button.
  7. Here is the generated file after confirming the Save File Form:

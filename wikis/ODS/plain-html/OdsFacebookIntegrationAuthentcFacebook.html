Authenticate in ODS using your Facebook account

  1. Make sure the steps from the ODS Facebook Integration Administrators Guide are performed.
  2. Make sure you have linked ODS user with your Facebook account.
  3. Go to http://cname:port/ods .



  4. Click "Sign In".



  5. Go to "Facebook" tab.



  6. Click the "Connect" button.
  7. Enter your Facebook account credentials in the new opened window and click "Login":



  8. As result ODS will indicate the established connection:



  9. Click the "Facebook Login" button.
  10. As result a successful log in should be performed and you should be presented with the ODS home page logged in:

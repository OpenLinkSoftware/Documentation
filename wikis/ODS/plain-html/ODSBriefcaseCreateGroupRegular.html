Creating Regular User Group in ODS-Briefcase

Here is a sample scenario how to create Regular User Group in ODS-Briefcase:

  1. Log in in http://cname:port/ods.
  2. Go to Briefcase.
  3. Go to Groups.
  4. Click "Create".
  5. Enter in the shown form group name and short description:



  6. Go to "Members".



  7. Click the "Add" button.
  8. Enter list of ODS users:



  9. Click "Create".
  10. As result your new group will be created.

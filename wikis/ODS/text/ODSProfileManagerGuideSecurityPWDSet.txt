---+Password Settings

In the *Password Settings* tab, the ODS user can change his/her password:

   1. In the presented form, enter the old password once and then enter the new one twice:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/p11.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1. Click "Change".
   1. After successfully changed the password, the following message should be presented:
<verbatim>
The password was changed successfully.
</verbatim>

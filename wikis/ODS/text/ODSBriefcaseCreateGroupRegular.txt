---++Creating Regular User Group in ODS-Briefcase

Here is a sample scenario how to create Regular User Group in ODS-Briefcase:

   1 Log in in http://cname:port/ods.
   1 Go to Briefcase.
   1 Go to Groups.
   1 Click "Create".
   1 Enter in the shown form group name and short description:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/rg1.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to "Members".
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/rg2.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click the "Add" button.
   1 Enter list of ODS users:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/rg3.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click "Create".
   1 As result your new group will be created.

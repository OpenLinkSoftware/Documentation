---+++<nowiki>OpenID2</nowiki> Register ODS User

   1. Go to http://demo.openlinksw.com/ods .
   1. Click the "Sign Up" link.
   1. Go to tab "OpenID".
   1. For OpenID Url enter "https://me.yahoo.com/myodsdemo" .
   1. Check the check-box "I agree to the Terms of Service."
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/o1.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. Click the "Sign Up" button.
   1. As result the Yahoo page with "Continue" button will be shown. Click it.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/o2.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. As result will be redirected to ODS Sign Up page with this message shown:
<verbatim>
Your openID provider has not supplied your nickname and e-mail address. 
</verbatim>
   1. Enter in the shown form Login Name for ex.: myodsdemo and E-Mail for ex.: myodsdemo@openlinksw.com
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/o3.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. Click the "Sign Up" button.
   1. As result the user *myodsdemo* will be created.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/o4.png" style="wikiautogen"/>

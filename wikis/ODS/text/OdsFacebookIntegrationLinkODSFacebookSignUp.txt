---+Link ODS user with Facebook account using the Sign Up Facebook feature

   1 Make sure the steps from the [[OdsFacebookIntegrationSetupAdminGuide][ODS Facebook Integration Administrators Guide]] are performed.
   1 Go to http://cname:port/ods  .
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f1.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Click "Sign Up".
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f2.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to "Facebook" tab.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f3.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Click the "Connect" button
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f4.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Enter in Facebook window form your Facebook credentials and click Login:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f5a.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to the Facebook tab
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f5b.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Click "Sign Up". 
   1 As result will be created a new ODS user and the link between ODS user and Facebook user will be established.

---++Export in ODS Bookmarks Manager

ODS Bookmarks Manager offers the bookmarks to be exported by format:
   * Netscape  
   * XBEL

Here is a sample scenario:

   1. Go to http://cname:port/ods
   1. Log in as user.
   1. Go to Bookmarks
   1. Click the "Export" button from the Bookmarks Manager Main horizontal navigation.
   1. Choose a Format and enter Filename
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/eb.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. Click the "Export" button.
   1. As result a form for saving the file will be shown and the file will be generated.

---+Authenticate in ODS using your Facebook account


   1 Make sure the steps from the [[OdsFacebookIntegrationSetupAdminGuide][ODS Facebook Integration Administrators Guide]] are performed.
   1 Make sure you have [[OdsFacebookIntegrationLinkODSFacebook][linked ODS user with your Facebook account]].
   1 Go to http://cname:port/ods  .
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f1.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1. Click "Sign In".
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f8.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to "Facebook" tab.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f10.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 Click the "Connect" button.
   1 Enter your Facebook account credentials in the new opened window and click "Login":
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f10a.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1 As result ODS will indicate the established connection:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f11.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%
   1. Click the "Facebook Login" button. 
   1 As result a successful log in should be performed and you should be presented with the ODS home page logged in:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/f12.png" style="wikiautogen"/>%BR%%BR%

---++ ODS Wiki Cluster Administration

---+++ Create a Wiki cluster
   1 Using a web browser, login into you ODS user account, for example, <code><nowiki>&lt;http://myopenlink/ods/&gt;</nowiki></code>.
   1 Select the Wiki tab from the ODS Bar.
   1 Select the "Create New" Link.
   1 Enter your Wiki name, for example, <code><nowiki>myWiki</nowiki></code>.
   1 If you want to change the URL you see, just click the button "Change" next to the URL and write the new path.
   1 Click the button "Create and Launch Application", which will create a new Wiki instance (also called a "Wiki Cluster") 
<code><nowiki>myWiki</nowiki></code>.
   1 To customize your Wiki, look in [[ODSWikiClusterSettings][Cluster Settings]].

---+++iCalendar Example for Extracting Metadata and DET based resources generation

Here is sample scenario:

   1. Go to http://&lt;cname&gt;/ods
   1 Log in as user
   1 Go to Briefcase
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/b8.png" />%BR%%BR%  
   1 Upload a iCalendar file, for ex. Work.ics
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/b9.png" />%BR%%BR%  
   1 Now <b>to view the retrieved metadata</b>, click for the uploaded resource the "Update Properties" icon from the Action column.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/b10.png" />%BR%%BR%  
   1 Go to "Metadata"
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/b11.png" />%BR%%BR%

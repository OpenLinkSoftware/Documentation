---++Downstreaming (Importing) in Weblog

Downstreaming in Weblog can be performed from Settings->Downstream(Import).

---+++Sample Scenario 

Here is sample scenario:

   1 Install ODS and Weblog packages.
   1 Login at http://host:port/ods
   1 Go to Weblog from the ODS main vertical left navigation. 
   1 Go to Settings->Downstream(Import).
   1. Enter for URL an rss for ex. http://www.openlinksw.com/blog/~kidehen/gems/rss.xml
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/imb1.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1 Click the "Next" button.
   1 Confirm with "Yes"
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/imb2.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1 As result will be shown message with number of imported posts.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/imb3.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1 Go to "Home" in order to view the imported posts.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/imb4.png" style="wikiautogen"/>

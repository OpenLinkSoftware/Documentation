Understanding the member model of the ODS Wiki application

ODS-Wiki defines special groups and users to give different access rights to Wiki Clusters for ODS users and guests:

  1. The following are defined at cluster creation:
    • WikiAdmin — members of the group can read, write, revert, and delete articles and clusters.
    • WikiGuest — everyone not logged in to ODS.
    • WikiUser — members of the group are all ODS users with Wiki instance.
  2. For each new Wiki instance two groups are defined :
    • myWikiReaders — members of the group can read the articles in myWiki cluster
    • myWikiWriters — members of the group can read, write, revert, and delete all articles in myWiki cluster

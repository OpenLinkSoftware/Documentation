---++Creating WebID User Group in ODS

Here is a sample scenario how to create WebID Protocol User Group in ODS:

   1 Log in in http://cname:port/ods
   1 Go to Profile Edit -> ACL Groups
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i9.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click "Add".
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i10.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Enter in the shown form group name and short description:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i11.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to "WebIDs" tab:
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i5.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click the "Add" button
   1 Enter Existing Personal WebID, for ex. http://ods-qa.openlinksw.com/dataspace/person/tom#this
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i6.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 In order to add new Personal WebID, click again the "Add" button
   1 Enter another Personal WebID, for ex. http://ods-qa.openlinksw.com/dataspace/person/anna#this
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i7.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click "Create"
   1 As result your WebID group will be created with IRI:  http://<cname>/dataspace/user-name/group/group-name ( so in our example it is: http://ods-qa.openlinksw.com/dataspace/demo/group/MyWebIDGroup )  and shown in the list of current WebIDs groups
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/i8.png" />%BR%%BR%

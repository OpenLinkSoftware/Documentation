---++Set Up X.509 Certificate Service

Setting Up a X.509 Certificate Service URL allows for users to use the OpenLink X.509 Certificate Generation Wizard:

   1. Log in at ODS (http://cname:port/ods) as admin user
   1 Go to Site Settings -> Server Settings
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/s14.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Enter for "X.509 Certificate Service" the following URL:
<verbatim>
http://rnd.openlinksw.com/certgen/CertGen.application
</verbatim>
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/s15.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click "Set"

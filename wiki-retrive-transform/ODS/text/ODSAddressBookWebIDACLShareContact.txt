---+ ODS AddressBook WebID Protocol Share Contact

   1 Install [[https://virtuoso.openlinksw.com/download/][ODS AddressBook]] VAD package.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac10.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to http://cname:port/ods and register user, for ex. with name demo and password demo.
      * Note: you can also use an existing ODS user for the next steps
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac11.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Logged in as user demo, click the link "AddressBook" from the left vertical navigation.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac12.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click the "New contact" icon from the main ODS AddressBook horizontal navigation or use the "Import" feature in order to add contact(s) .
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac13.png" />%BR%%BR%
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac14.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Suppose a new Contact with name "Tim Berners Lee" is created.
   1 The contact should be shown in the list of contacts for user demo.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac16.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Click the "edit" link for the contact "Tim Berners Lee".
   1 The Main properties tab for the contact should be shown.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac15.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1 Go to the "Sharing" tab
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ac17.png" />%BR%%BR%
      * To share the contact to a person, see details [[ODSAddressBookShareContactToPerson][here]].
      * To share the contact to group of users, see details [[ODSAddressBookShareContactToGroup][here]].
      * To share the contact to public, see details [[ODSAddressBookShareContactToPublic][here]].

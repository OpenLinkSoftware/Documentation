---++Importing in Bookmarks

You can import your bookmarks using the Import feature of the ODS Bookmarks by:
   * Source type:
      1. File
      1. WebDAV
      1. URI
      1. del.icio.us

Here is a sample scenario:

   1. Go to http//demo.openlinksw.com/ods
   1. Login as demo with password demo
   1. Go to Bookmarks
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/bi1.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. Click the "Import" button from the Main Horizontal Bookmarks Navigation.
   1. Click the "Next" button.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/bi2.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. Click the "Browse" button and select a bookmark file from your local file system.
   1. Click the "Next" button.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/bi3.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. In the shown form:
      1. You can enter a desired folder the bookmarks to be imported too or leave this field empty. For ex. you can value "test"
      2. You can add tag(s) for ex.: new or to chose from the shown list of "My Tags".
   1. Click the "Import" button.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/bi4.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. As result will be shown the window with progress bar. Here you can:
      1. Leave the process until the import finishes to 100%.
      1. Click the "Background" button, which will cause the process to run in background mode.
      1. Click the "Stop" button which will stop the import process.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/bi5.png" style="wikiautogen"/>
   1. As result the bookmarks will be imported.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/bi6.png" style="wikiautogen"/>

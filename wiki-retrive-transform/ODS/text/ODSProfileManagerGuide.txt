---+ODS Profile Manager Usage Guide

The following guide shows how to manage your ODS User Profile.

%TOC% 


---++ Accessing the ODS Profile Manager

A logged-in user may access the ODS Profile Manager at any time through the link at the top of the page.

   1. Visit an ODS instance with your Web browser, &lt;[[http://host:port/ods][http://host:port/ods]]&gt;, such as [[http://my.openlinksw.com/ods][http://my.openlinksw.com/ods]].
   1. Log in or register as an ODS user.  (If you have ever registered with or downloaded from <nop>OpenLink Software, you have an existing account which uses your email address as the username. You can [[http://download.openlinksw.com:8080/download/register.vsp?hname=http://download.openlinksw.com:8080/download/main_login.vsp&nhdr=][register now or get a password reminder]], if needed.)
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/p1.png" />%BR%%BR%
   1. Successful log-in and/or registration will bring you to the ODS user home page.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/p2.png" />%BR%%BR%   
   1 Click the *Edit* link (next to, but not the same as, the *Profile* link) in the header bar, to access the ODS Profile Manager.
%BR%%BR%<img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/p3.png" />%BR%%BR%

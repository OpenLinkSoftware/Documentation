---+++Export Subscriptions in ODS Feed Manager

ODS Feed Manager offers Subscriptions to be exported to files with type:
   * OCS
   * OPML
   * FOAF

Here is a sample scenario:

   1. Go to http://cname:port/ods
   1. Log in as user.
   1. Go to Feed Manager
   1. Go to Administration
   1. Click the "Export".
   1. Click on one of the OCS, OPML or FOAF type links.
      * <img src="%ATTACHURLPATH%/ef.png" style="wikiautogen"/>   
   1. As result the export file will be generated.

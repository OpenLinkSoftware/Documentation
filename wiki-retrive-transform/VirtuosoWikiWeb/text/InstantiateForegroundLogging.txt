==Logging & Tracing==

===Instantiate Foreground Logging===

Start Virtuoso on the command and pass -f or +foreground in order to instantiate foreground logging. For example:

{{{
 .\virtuoso.exe +foreground
 .\virtuoso.exe -f
}}}

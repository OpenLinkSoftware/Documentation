==Working With Amazon EC2 AMI Instances &S3 Buckets==

===Can the EC2 Extension VAD package be installed on a non-AMI Virtuoso instance?===

Yes, the EC2 Extension VAD can be installed on any Virtuoso  
installation, and can be downloaded from:

[[http://download.openlinksw.com/packages/5.0/virtuoso/ec2exts_dav.vad]]

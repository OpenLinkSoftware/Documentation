Gathering Additional Evidence


OpenLink License File

OpenLink Account Managers generate licenses for registered OpenLink products. These licenses may have a 4 digit name. Alternatively, they may be named for the product which they register. Moreover, OpenLink's current licenses have .lic extension. Older product licenses have .ini extensions. Here are some sample license names:

5465.ini - 3.2 License
oplrqb.ini - 3.2 License
7321.lic - 4.0 License
oplrqb.lic - 4.0 License

Finally, licenses should appear in the /bin sub-directory of Multi-Tier or Virtuoso server component installations. Single-Tier licenses typically appear in the Lite32 sub-directory of Single-Tier client installations.

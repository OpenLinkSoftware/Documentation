=== Unknown or null DBMS connection startup argument. (-31000) 
 
"Unknown or null DBMS connection startup argument. (-31000)" is an Ingres specific error message. It occurs when users do not pass the database name in the Data Source Name. To resolve this problem, insure that this name is passed.

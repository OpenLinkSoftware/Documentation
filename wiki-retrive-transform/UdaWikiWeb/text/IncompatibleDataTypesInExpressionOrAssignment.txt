===Incompatible data types in expression or assignment===


The "Incompatible data types in expression or assignment" error message is a Progress error message associated with dates. If you are using dates in your queries, apply the following date escape syntax: {d 'yyyy-mm-dd'}. If your queries do not involve dates, contact <nop>OpenLink Product Support

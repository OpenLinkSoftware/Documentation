===[Protocol SPX]===

This section is only relevant, and required when using the SPX protocol.

|**Parameter** |	**Default** |	**Comment**|
|<nop>SAPBroadcast| 	Yes| 	Broadcast SAP registration packets|
|<nop>SAPInterval |	100 	|Number of seconds between each SAP broadcast|
|<nop>SAPServiceType| 	0x321F 	|Type of the SAP service|
|<nop>SAPServiceName| 	OPENLINK |	name of the SAP service|
|<nop>HostFile 	|HOSTS.IPX 	|For IPX/SPX name to address translation|

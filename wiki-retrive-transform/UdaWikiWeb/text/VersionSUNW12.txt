===Version 'SUNW_1.2' not found (required by file bin/w3config/setup)===

"Version 'SUNW_1.2'" errors occur when Solaris 2.7 software is installed on Solaris 2.5 or 2.6 operating systems. If you have a Solaris 2.5 or 2.6 operating system, install <nop>OpenLink's Solaris 2.5 software.

==Understanding the Informix native client ==

The Informix native client is an IBM product that provides access to Informix databases via the Informix proprietary communications protocol. At the time of writing, this product is called INFORMIX-Connect. However, marketing names are subject to change. In fact, the same product is often referred to as Informix-Net or I-Net.

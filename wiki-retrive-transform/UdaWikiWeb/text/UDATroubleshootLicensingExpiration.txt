==Licensing==

===Existing license key expired ===

You are entitled to two, 15 day evaluation keys. If your first key has expired, you may download an additional key from the <nop>OpenLink Web site. There are two ways to do this. You can go through the downloads matrix. At the end of the matrix, you can download a license without downloading additional software. Alternatively, you can contact <nop>OpenLink Product Support or Account Management for an additional key. %BR%
%BR%
Once you have consumed both licenses, you will need to acquire permanent, commercial licensing.Contact your regional <nop>OpenLink Account Manager to obtain this licensing. Product Support can not provide commercial or non-expiring license keys.

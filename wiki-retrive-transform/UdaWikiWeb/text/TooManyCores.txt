= Number of CPUs too high for license =

Our drivers are licensed by physical processor core on the host machine. Use our [[http://support.openlinksw.com/supportweb/DetectCPU|CPU Detection Help]] page to detect your physical cores. 

[[http://support.openlinksw.com/support/online-support.vsp|Contact Product Support]] if you have problems in environments with virtual processors, including Solaris Containers, AIX LPARs, and Intel <nop>HyperThreading.

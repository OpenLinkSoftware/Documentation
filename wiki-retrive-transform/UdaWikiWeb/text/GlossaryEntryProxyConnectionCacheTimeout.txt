==<nop>ProxyConnectionCacheTimeout==

The <nop>ProxyConnectionCacheTimeout parameter appears in the [<nop>HTTPServer] section of the virtuoso.ini file. This parameter specifies the number of seconds which Virtuoso will wait, before terminating idle connections to other HTTP servers. Such connections may result from http_get, SOAP client functions, and proxying. 

Example: 

{{{
ProxyConnectionCacheTimeout = 15 
}}}

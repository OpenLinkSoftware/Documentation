==<nop>ServerEnable==

The <nop>ServerEnable parameter appears in the [Replication] section of the virtuoso.ini file. You must set <nop>ServerEnable to 1 if you want your server instance to publish transactionally replicated data. 

Example: 

{{{
ServerEnable = 1 
}}}

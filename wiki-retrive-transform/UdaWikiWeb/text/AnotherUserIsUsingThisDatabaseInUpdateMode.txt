===Another user is using this database in update mode===

"Another user" warnings are associated with Progress authentication problems. If you receive "another user" messages, remove usernames and passwords from your ODBC DSNs or JDBC connection strings. Then, test.
    
===Evidence===

* Session Rules Book
* Request Broker Log

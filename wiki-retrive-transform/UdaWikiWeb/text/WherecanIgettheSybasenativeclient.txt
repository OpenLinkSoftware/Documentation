==Obtain the Sybase native client (a.k.a "<nop>OpenClient") ==

<nop>OpenLink Software does not supply the Sybase native client. Instead, the Sybase native client should ship with the standard Sybase DBMS install media.

If you do not have your Sybase install media, contact Sybase.

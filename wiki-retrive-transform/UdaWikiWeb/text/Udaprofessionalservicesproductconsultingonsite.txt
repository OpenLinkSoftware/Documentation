  On Site 
 [[OpenLink]]  Software provides both On-Site and Off-Site consultancy aimed at meeting the needs of customers. 

With On-Site consultancy, [[OpenLink]]  Consultant(s) will visit and work from your offices of choice. We understand your organization might require [[OpenLink]]  Software to be at your site and we will consider this for one of the following reasons: 

*To work as part of a team.  
*Cost-effectiveness.  
*Custom training.  
*Custom Development using your environment.

==Obtaining the Progress native client (a.k.a "Progress® Client Networking" or "Progress® SQL-92 Client Access") ==

<nop>OpenLink Software cannot supply the Progress native client. 

The Progress native client typically ships on the standard Progress DBMS install media, and is generally one of several installation options that you can select. However, packaging and marketing labels are subject to change.

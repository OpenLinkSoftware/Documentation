==Glossary of Technical Terms==

==Database Server==

The Database Server field appears in the ODBC Data Sources Administrator. You may pass any valid, database specific connection parameters in your Database Server field. For instance, you may pass Progress sockets options. Alternatively, you may pass -S server_name to designate a particular <nop>SQLServer service. 

See Also: Connect Options, Options

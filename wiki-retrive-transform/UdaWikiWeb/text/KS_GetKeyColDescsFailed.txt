===KS_<nop>GetKeyColDescs failed===

This error is associated with <nop>OpenLInk's OLE DB Rowset Viewer application. This error occurs when the target table does not have a unique index or primary key. The UDA cursor library needs this in order to manage the rowsets in Rowset Viewer.

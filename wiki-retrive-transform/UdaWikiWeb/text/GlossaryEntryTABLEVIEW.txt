==TABLEVIEW==

The TABLEVIEW variable occurs in the [Environment PROGRESSXX] section of your <nop>OpenLink Session Rules Book. If you use tableview, you must set TABLEVIEW to the absolute path to your tableview .dat file.   

Note: This variable may be set and exported from your Administrative Assistant's Database Agent Settings Wizard or Form. 

Example: 

{{{
TABLEVIEW=/dbs/progress/tableview.dat 
}}}
